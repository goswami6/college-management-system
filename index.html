About us

Welcome to Modern IAS Academy, a premier institute dedicated to nurturing aspirants for the Indian Administrative Service. Established in 2023, our institute has quickly become a beacon of excellence and bringing innovation in fulfilling demand of civil services examinations.

Our founding ethos is rooted in providing quality education and holistic development to IAS aspirants. We believe that clearing IAS examination requires more than just academic knowledge; it demands a blend of strategic learning, analytical thinking and a deep understanding of societal issues. At Modern IAS Academy, we strive to foster these qualities in every student.

Our Vision

At Modern IAS Academy, our vision is to be a leader in providing mentorship for civil services examinations, empowering the next generation of civil servants who will drive positive change in India. We aspire to create an environment where the pursuit of academic excellence and ethical governance converge, fostering a breed of efficient, empathetic and innovative civil service leaders. Our aim is to not only assist students in succeeding in the examination but also to instill in them the values and skills necessary to excel in public service.
